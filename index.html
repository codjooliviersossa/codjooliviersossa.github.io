Dr. Codjo Olivier SOSSA

Statistical and Economist

My name is Codjo Olivier Sossa, I have a PhD in Statistics from the Graduate Program in Statistics at the Federal University of Pernambuco (UFPE). I have a Master's degree in Economics from the Graduate Program in Economics (PIMES/UFPE). I have a degree in Economics with a specialization in Statistics-Econometrics from the Faculty of Economic and Management Sciences of the University of Abomey-Calavi (FASEG-UAC). I have worked in academia as a substitute teacher since 2024. I currently teach at the State University of Feira de Santana (UEFS). I also work in economics and statistics, with an emphasis on international economics and inferential statistics. I am a specialist, advisor and consultant in data processing, analysis, monitoring and evaluation of projects and reports. I work as a researcher on Afro-indigenous issues in Brazil and as a Statistical Advisor for studies and projects at the Collective of Pankararu Indigenous Families of Petrolândia (COFIPPE). I am also President of the Beninese Diaspora Association in Brazil.
