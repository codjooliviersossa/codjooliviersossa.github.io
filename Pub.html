UFPE's Internationalization Program

It was a very special day when I learned that I had been selected to take part in the Internalization Program at the Federal University of Pernambuco in 2016. A program that allows 03 foreigners to enter the largest course in Economics in the Department of Applied Social Sciences (PIMES), one of the largest programs in Brazil. Despite the difficulties I encountered with the Portuguese language, I managed to overcome them for an even greater challenge that the Economics course provided. It was very challenging, but with my head on straight and with a lot of dedication, I managed to pass the Master's and enter the PhD program, but this time in Statistics.
